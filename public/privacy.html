Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

FocusBro is designed as a browser-first productivity app. By default, your notes, tasks, timer history, and preferences are stored locally in your browser.

Data we process

  • Local app state in your browser storage (for product functionality).
  • Basic technical logs through our hosting provider for security and reliability.
  • Advertising data via Google AdSense when ads are displayed.

Advertising and cookies

We use Google AdSense to show ads. Google and its partners may use cookies or device identifiers to personalize ads and measure performance. You can manage ad personalization in your Google Ads Settings.

Third-party services

  • Google AdSense (advertising)
  • Cloudflare (content delivery and security)

Your choices

  • You can clear FocusBro local data at any time in your browser settings.
  • You can opt out of personalized advertising through Google controls.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact us at support@focusbro.net.
